## What is this?
A mobile-first retail showcase with product cards and WhatsApp 'Chat to Order' flow.

## Add to your portfolio
1) Upload the **demos/retail** folder to your site repo (path: `/demos/retail/index.html`).
2) Upload **assets/retail-demo.svg** to your main site's `assets/` folder.
3) In your main `index.html`, paste the snippet from `index.with-retail.snippet.html` where you list your work.

## Hosting notes
- Works on Netlify and GitHub Pages. WhatsApp links are prefilled with item name & price.
- Customize product list in the `<script>` array.
